Help!

Create a custom path pickin up locations through tags. You cannot go to the Map before you haven't selected any location.

You must give access to the GPS in order to use this app, but do not worry: we will not save anykind of data.

At the top of the interface, click on Map to see the path you created.

Go to the locations and scan the QR code you will find on the yellow signs.
Click on the button "" which is on the map interface, it will open a popup: click on "select file" and you will be able to take a photo: shoot the QR and confirm it. ✓

Pay attention! You have to be very close to the qr code when you scan it.

At each location, you will generate a part of a speculative poem :)

If you want to modify the locations you can go back to the menu through the arrow you can find on the map interface (🡠) or modify directly the path clicking on the markers on the map.

If the app seems blocked you could have cookies problems: activate the cookies storing on the settings of your browser.

Delete the cookies to restart the game when you want.

Caution Weird Floor
An XPUB Geocatching Game

Conceptualised, designed and developed by Federico Poni and Martin Foucaut from the master Experimental Publishing, Piet Zwart Institute for The Hague Contemporary Art Weekend 2021.

To report an issue, please contact us.

The repo of this project can be found here.
We use cookies only for store locally player's choices instead to use databases: we don't get your data.

We used those libraries:
leaflet.js
html5-qrcode
Sweetalert
js-cookies

and we would like to thanks the open source community.
